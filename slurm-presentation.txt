Slurm is a workload manager and job scheduler
Slurm has partitions and accounts
Partitions are logic unit which break the cluster
Account are the access groups

Three main functions: 
Allocate resources (exclusively and non-exclusively).
Framework for job management.
Arbitrates contention for resources through queing pending work.

Compute nodes (slurmd) and management nodes (slurmctl). 
Partitions group nodes.
